1.1.1 By Q2 of 2011 Timor-Leste will finalize rapid assessment and mapping of marine resources (coral reef, mangroves, sea grass, etc.) to define and identify coastal and marine priority areas.

Significant habitat and biodiversity studies have been undertaken since 2009, with generous support from partners such as the Australian and United States Governments, Charles Darwin University and the Australian Museum. Further biodiversity surveys are needed for the north-west coast of the country, with a particular focus on proposed MPA sites in Atauro Island and Batugede.
