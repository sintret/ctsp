3.1.4 By Q4 of 2014 Timor-Leste will start its discussion with the Indonesian Government on the establishment of a trans-boundary protected area network.

Â© Matthew Abbott / CTSP

The Government of Timor-Leste is pursuing the establishment of Marine Protected Areas which have been identified as a priority under the Lesser Sunda MPA design. This will enable the establishment of a transboundary network of MPAs in the Lesser Sunda region. Opportunities for cooperation between Timor-Leste and Indonesia have been discussed by government staff through regional fora such as the Arafura Timor Seas Ecosystem Action Project (ATSEA), and the CTI. With acknowledgement of the value of managing at marine ecosystems at larger scales, interest in supporting this work has been expressed by some foundations and bilateral partners. Partnerships will continue to be pursued into the future.
